Printing Services Frequently Asked Questions

What is printing services?

umaiboz Store offers a wide variety of printing and finishing services, including electronic file access (e.g., e-mails, CDs, USB drives)

What is digital printing?

Digital printing assembles the images for print from a complex set of numbers and formulas. These images are captured from pixels, and the digitalised image is used to control the deposition of ink to replicate the image you would like to print.

How can I get my print job once it is finished?

You can take it right from one of our offices or choose the delivery method that suits you best.

Can I get a price quote for my print job?
